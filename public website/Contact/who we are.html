Minds to One, formerly Talcott, is a small business working on concept mapping. The Ariadne software has been developed in the ‘90’s and it has evolved over time into a web based service and all procedures can be performed using the internet. The software is constantly developing; this means that there are great possibilities to modify the software based the feedback of the users. We adjust our services to your desires!

 

Throughout internet you can organize your Ariadne sessions completely autonomous. However, Minds to One can also arrange the complete concept mapping session for you. We have 15 fast laptops with a large screen, which facilitates grouping and prioritizing the statements. Furthermore, it is possible to let us arrange location, coffee/ tea and some lunch or sweets or even get our full support in interpreting the results.

 

 

Peter Severens is director and owner of Minds to One, educated in mathematics and sociology. He was introduced to concept mapping in the mental health care and he developed Ariadne.
